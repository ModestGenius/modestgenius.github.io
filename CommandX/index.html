Send emails, delete files, and more... Right from your XMod Pro Template!

CommandX extends the functionality of the <xmod:CommandButton> within templates, allowing you to easily send templated emails, delete files and directories, and even send silent posts.
Documentation

Ridiculously Simple

It's just an <xmod:CommandButton> on steroids!

Send Email

Think of a follow button, or anything else for that matter where you'd want to send an instant email notification.

Delete Files

When deleting a record from the database, you can also delete any associated files. Optionally remove entire directories.

Silent Post

Optionally send a silent post request to the URL of your choice, and assign as many field parameters as you'd like!

Combine Actions

Similar to form actions, you can chain multiple actions together, which will execute in the order in which they're placed.
