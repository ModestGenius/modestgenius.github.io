Accept payments from anyone... anywhere!

Stripe for XMod Pro is a collection of custom form actions and template controls that interact with the Stripe API. No setup fees, merchant accounts or gateways! In short, accepting credit card payments on your site couldn't be easier!
Documentation

Get Started In Minutes

A starter form is included to get you started quickly, and the documentation has a ton of examples!

Create Customers

Includes a custom form action to create customers, attach a credit card to that customer, and more!

Accept Payments

Use the single click checkout control, or create an advanced form for complex payment scenarios.

Store the Data

Optionally specify stored procedures to push Stripe's response to your own database.

Secure

Credit card details never have to touch your server, eliminating the worries of PCI-Compliance.



Multiple Sellers

You can override the ApiKey on a control by control basis, so accepting payments from more than one account is easy!

No Redirect

Customers stay directly on your site... No need to be sent to a third party page to collect a payment!

Multiple Currencies

Stripe supports a ridiculous amount of currencies all over the world. See this page.

Conditional Action

Every form action has a property that you can set, determining execution. This can be hard-coded, or a field token!
