Clickatell Form Action for XMod Pro

Send SMS/Text Messages with an XMod Pro Form using the Clickatell platform.

Simple, yet flexible

The Clickatell Form Action is an extension for XMod Pro that enables you to easily send SMS messages utilizing your Clickatell account, after a form submission. The installation includes an example form. Simply set your Clickatell credentials and you're all set!
